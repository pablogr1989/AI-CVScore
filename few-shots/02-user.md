About the position
The QA Test Engineer at Abbott is responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of software products within the Diabetes division, focusing on mobile apps. This role involves developing and optimizing manual and automated tests, conducting thorough testing processes, and collaborating with team members to enhance automation practices. The position is integral to maintaining compliance with medical industry standards while driving innovation in diabetes management technologies.

Responsibilities
Perform testing on a wide range of mobile devices (different brands and models), creating and updating datasets for automation scripts.
Conduct Bluetooth and NFC compatibility testing across various devices and operating systems.
Tailor manual testing solutions to meet the specific needs of the medical industry, with a strong focus on regulatory compliance and operational efficiency.
Identify opportunities to improve testing processes, implementing innovative solutions to increase efficiency and reliability.
Produce clear, high-quality documentation for all testing systems and updates, ensuring compliance with medical industry standards.
Develop and maintain automation scripts using Java or Python.
